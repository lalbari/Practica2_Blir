AMSTERDAM ZOO

2021

Category: Illustration

Every Saturday in July and August, ARTIS Zoo in Amsterdam stays open until sunset for Summer Nights. During the golden hours, the park comes alive with music and food trucks.

They invited us to create illustrated picnic blankets full of animal trivia as a gift and promotion. Four of these made a complete design and visitors had to connect with each other to get the big picture. These illustrations expanded to multiple concepts like a scavenger hunt, merchandise and decoration of the park.
